Understanding the Concept

Finding what percent X is of Y means determining how much X contributes to Y as a percentage. In our example, 40 out of 200 is what percentage?

Manual Calculation Steps

  1. Divide X by Y: 40 / 200 = 0.2.
  2. Multiply by 100: 0.2 * 100 = 20%.
  3. Result: 40 is 20% of 200.
