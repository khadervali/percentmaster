What Does X% of Y Mean?

When you need to find X% of Y, you’re calculating a percentage of a number. For example, what is 25% of 200? This means you’re looking for 25% (or 25/100) of the total value, 200.

Manual Calculation Steps

  1. Convert the Percentage to a Decimal: Divide X by 100. For 25%, that’s 25 / 100 = 0.25.
  2. Multiply by Y: Multiply the decimal by the number Y. So, 0.25 * 200 = 50.
  3. Result: 25% of 200 is 50.
