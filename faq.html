Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers!

1. Is PercentMaster free to use?

Yes, our percentage calculator is completely free for personal use.

2. Do you store my calculations?

No, all calculations are performed locally in your browser. We don’t collect or store your data.

3. Can I use this on my phone?

Absolutely! Our site is mobile-friendly and works on any device with a modern browser.

4. What if I get an error?

Ensure you’ve entered valid numbers in all fields. Contact us if issues persist.

5. Can I suggest new features?

We’d love to hear from you! Email suggestions to support@percentmaster.com.
